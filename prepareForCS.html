How to Prepare for Computer Science

Get familar with computers

Getting familiar with computers will help you in multiple ways during your computer science degree. Computer science at its very core is using computers. So, getting the basics, like typing, understanding how to navigate different operating systems, and useful shortcuts down will be a great start to your journey in computer science.

Taking computer classes is another great way to familiarize yourself with proper computer usage. This would be a benefical step for any potential computer science student. Local comunity colleges often offer these types of classes.
