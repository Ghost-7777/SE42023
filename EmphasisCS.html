    Emphases Within CS

    Being a Computer Science major can open more doors for your future than you might expect! Computer Science majors at most universities offer different emphases based on the students passions! Listed below are just some emphases that a CS student can do!

    • Cybersecurity
    • Business
    • Gaming and Animation
    • Graduate programs

    Cybersecurity

  • With an emphases in cybersecurity you will not take some classes that just Computer Science majors take such as calculus. Cybersecurity allows you to take 15 credit hours of regular cyber classes and 4 total credit hours of cyber team oriented classes.
