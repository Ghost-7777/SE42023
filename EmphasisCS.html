Emphases Within CS

Being a Computer Science major can open more doors for your future than you might expect! Computer Science majors here at Oklahoma Christian University offer different emphasis based on the students passions! Listed below are just some emphases that a CS student can do!

  • Cybersecurity
  • Business
  • Gaming and Animation
  • Dual undergraduate/Graduate degrees

Cybersecurity

  • With an emphasis in cybersecurity you will not take some classes that just Computer Science majors take such as calculus. Here at OC having an emphases in cybersecurity allows you to take 15 credit hours of regular cyber classes and 4 total credit hours of cyber team oriented classes.

Business

  • With an emphasis in business it will lead you to take courses that are the same ones business majors take also. Here at OC you will take 15 credit hours of your choosing from a list of business courses.

Gaming and Animation

  • Being a gaming and animation computer science major you will have five courses adding up to 15 credit hours specifically dealing with gaming/animation here at OC.
