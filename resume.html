Tips to write a Computer Science Resume

  1. Personal Information - Name and contact information.
  2. Education - Name of the University and your major.
  3. Work Experience - If you do not have much work experience yet, you can include projects that you have worked on.
  4. Skills - This may include technical tools you know how to use.
  5. Achievements/Certifications
